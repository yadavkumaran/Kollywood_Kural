Actors

Thalapathy Vijay

Full name : Joseph vijay chandrasekar

Birthday date : 02-02-2008

Birth place : Chennai, Tamil Nadu, India

Thalapathy Vijay is one of the most popular actors in Tamil cinema, known for his charismatic screen presence and dance skills. He has starred in numerous blockbuster films such as *Ghilli*, *Thuppakki*, *Mersal*, and *Master*. His fan base is immense, and he is often referred to as "Thalapathy" by his fans.

Recent film : GOAT,LEO,VARISU,BEAST,MASTER


Ajith Kumar

Full name : Ajith Kumar Subramaniam

Birthday date : 01-05-1971

Birth place : Secunderabad, Telangana, India

Ajith Kumar is a leading actor in Tamil cinema, admired for his versatility and screen presence. He is known for films like *Veeram*, *Vedalam*, *Viswasam*, and *Valimai*. He is also a passionate car racer and is fondly called "Thala" by his fans.

Recent film : gbu,vidamuyarchi,Thunivu, Valimai, Nerkonda Paarvai, Viswasam


Rajinikanth

Full name : Shivaji Rao Gaekwad

Birthday date : 12-12-1950

Birth place : Bangalore, Karnataka, India

Rajinikanth is a legendary actor in Indian cinema, known for his unique style and mass appeal. He has acted in iconic films like *Baashha*, *Sivaji*, *Enthiran*, and *Jailer*. He is revered as "Superstar" by his fans all over the world.

Recent film : Jailer, Annaatthe, Darbar, Petta


Kamal Haasan

Full name : Kamal Haasan
